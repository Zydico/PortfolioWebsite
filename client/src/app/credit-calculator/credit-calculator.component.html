Credit Score Calculator Group Project
March 2021 - May 2021
This was a group project taken during the ENGR 102 class of Spring 2021 at WVU. We were assigned into groups of 4 with the goal of the project being to use MATLAB and work individually to create a script that could calculate the credit of separate categories that would affect credit, and then combine them together into one calculator script.
I was personally responsible for how new credit and having multiple types of credit would affect your credit score. I also had to calculate and graph the payments and interest accumulated over time in the scenario where someone would have a history of late payments or not.
