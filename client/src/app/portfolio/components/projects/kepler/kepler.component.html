A 2D simulation of a Keplerian Orbit showcasing the 2 Body Problem with all the important variables. This is with the assumption that the time of periapsis passage is 0 seconds and the mass of the satellite is insignificant compared to the central body. The mass of the sun is assumed to be 1.989e+30 kg. 1 AU is assumed to be 149,598,000 km.
